Definition:

Postmenopausal Syndrome is the recurring symptoms experienced by women of age group 45-60 years, associated with complete stoppage of menstruation due to aging


Let's now have a look at the various symptoms of Postmenopausal Syndrome

Symptoms


The most common symptoms experienced by perimenopausal women are

Night Sweats

Hot Flashes

Mood Swings

Back Pain

Anxiety

UTI (Urinary Tract infection)

Irritability

Loss of libido
